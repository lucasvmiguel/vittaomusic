VITTAO FROM
BRAZIL

Originally from Brazil and now based in Amsterdam, VITTAO has been captivating audiences for over 10 years with his eclectic sound and dynamic performances. His signature style, which he calls "Dynamic House", is a carefully curated blend of various House subgenres, designed to create an immersive experience that takes the crowd on a vibrant journey through rhythm and melody. Often infused with Latin vibes, his sets resonate with energy and emotion, ensuring that every performance is memorable.

As a co-founder of the ROOTED Collective Amsterdam, VITTAO is deeply embedded in the local electronic music scene, fostering community and collaboration among artists and fans alike. The collective is known for its commitment to inclusivity and creativity, values that VITTAO embodies in his musical endeavors. His passion for music is not only evident in his live sets but also in his role as a curator of sound.

In addition to his performances, VITTAO hosts a monthly radio show called "Ritmo Dinâmico" on AMW Radio, where he shares his favorite tracks and connects with listeners on a deeper level. What sets this show apart is VITTAO's commitment to diversity; he always plays different tracks, never repeating one, allowing listeners to experience a fresh mix of sounds each time. Through this platform, he showcases emerging talent and celebrates the diverse influences that shape his sound. Whether behind the decks or on the airwaves, VITTAO is dedicated to creating memorable experiences that resonate with audiences and keep the spirit of dance music alive.
